  • Info: SFMOMABATHROOMS is an interactive and print experience showcasing the bathrooms located at the San Francisco Museum of Modern Art. These bathrooms extend the experience of the museum into liminal spaces that usually take no part in an experience of art.

  • JOSEFALBERSBOT
  • installation/twitter bot

  • Info: JOSEFALBERSBOT is a twitter bot that takes on the spirit of Bauhaus artist Josef Albers. With this twitter bot Alber’s existence in the 21st century is explored: with the ease of illustration and automation how would Albers go about creating his revolutionary pieces? Have his pieces ever held figurative meanings within their abstractions?

    Tweet a photo to @josefAlbersBot to make your own "Homage to the Square".

  • Roles: Interaction design, Coding, Art direction,
    Graphic design

    Materials: Twitter API, Processing, Node.js,
    iPad, Acrylic/Canvas

  • Timeline: 2 weeks
    October 2017
